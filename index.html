Three days on campus. Two days at home. One shared mission.

NCS is a Christ-centered hybrid school for Christian families who want the best of school, home, and community. We partner with parents — not to replace them — but to walk alongside them as they raise children in truth, wisdom, and faith. This is not a low-involvement model. It's a high-partnership one, for families who want to be deeply engaged.

How NCS Works

The Hybrid Advantage

3

Days on Campus

Classroom instruction, enrichment, chapel, and the friendships that come from learning together in community.

2

Days at Home

Structured home-learning days — not free days. Teachers provide lesson plans and remain available to guide families through the work.

1

Shared Mission

School and family in true partnership — raising children in truth, wisdom, and faith. Parents here are not spectators. They are partners.
