Our Beliefs

What We Stand For

MI

Moral Innocence

We protect the moral innocence of our students, allowing children to remain children — a core building block for healthy psychological and emotional development.

Tap to read more

Moral Innocence

We believe children should be allowed to remain children as long as possible. The school does not expose students to moral issues such as sex education or drug awareness — that responsibility belongs to parents at home.

". . . be wise in what is good, and innocent to what is evil."Romans 16:19b
OC

Others-Centered

We believe loving and serving others is not only a command of scripture, but an outflow of salvation.

Tap to read more

Others-Centered

Loving and serving others is both a command of scripture and an outflow of salvation. School life gives children daily opportunities to regard others above themselves and to reflect Christ in how they live.

". . . let each of you regard one another as more important than himself."Philippians 2:3b
